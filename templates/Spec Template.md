# Spec Template (Non-Software / Field Projects)

## 1) Purpose / Objectives
- Why this project exists.
- What success looks like (measurable outcomes).

## 2) Scope (In) and Exclusions (Out)
- In-scope items.
- Explicit exclusions.

## 3) Functional Requirements
- What the system must do in real-world use.

## 4) Non-Functional Requirements
- Runtime, durability, environmental limits, safety, portability, etc.

## 5) Constraints
- Budget, schedule, site access, power, materials, transport.

## 6) Assumptions
- Conditions you are relying on to stay true.

## 7) Deliverables
- Tangible outputs (installed system, documentation, diagrams).

## 8) Acceptance Criteria (SAT)
- Pass/fail checks and performance thresholds.

## 9) High-Level Architecture
- Block diagram style description of major subsystems and interfaces.

## 10) Risks (Spec-Level)
- Physical/field risks that shape requirements.

## 11) Open Decisions
- Key unresolved choices that must be made before execution.
